How to reset your Codewars account Progress

You can delete your account in order to reset your progress and sign back on with the same email.

Steps to Reset Your Codewars Progress

  1. Click on the options in the top right corner.
  2. Go to Account Settings.
  3. Scroll down and click on Close Account.
  4. Sign back in the same way you did before.
